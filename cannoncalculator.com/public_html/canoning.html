Hello, we are students from Borusan Vocational High School!

We, are a group of friends from Borusan Asım Kocabıyık Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School. We've met trigonometry through our mathematics classes, but realized we could test it outside of our books. And decided to explore how useful it is in real life.


We're here to learn and teach trigonometry

Our goal is to understand trigonometry not only in mathematical textbooks but also how we can use it in daily life. In particular, we are trying to learn how to apply trigonometry in practice, using the example of projectile motion.

In this project, we invite anyone who wants to explore and learn the real-world applications behind trigonometry. Perhaps we are not experts in mathematics, but we are open to learning and experiencing. If you also want to join this mathematical journey, join us!"
